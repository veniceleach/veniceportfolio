My Personal Development

Summary of the "Twelve Pillars":

  • Chapter 4: Success isn’t something we chase—it comes naturally when we focus on self-improvement.
  • Chapter 5: The people we surround ourselves with influence our mindset and success, so we must choose wisely.
  • Chapter 6: Waiting for the "perfect time" is a mistake—we need to take action despite fear.
  • Chapter 7: Success is about strong, meaningful relationships, not just achievements.

Reflection:

  • I've realized that I need to work on myself daily and stop waiting for the "perfect moment."
  • True success comes from improving my skills, taking action even when unsure, and surrounding myself with people who uplift me.
  • To apply this, I plan to set aside time each day to learn something new and stop delaying projects due to fear or doubt.
  • I will be more intentional about spending time with inspiring people and strengthening relationships with family, friends, and colleagues.
